About Us

Ribar Services is a business partnership bringing together the 30 years combined building industry experience of Ricardo Mateus and Barry Brindley.

Having worked together regularly - due to our complimentary skillsets, since 2015, Barry and Ricardo decided to officially form a partnership and offer their services as a business in 2021, and Ribar Services was born.

We are based in South West London and offer our services across the region and beyond into the surrounding London boroughs and home counties of Surrey and Kent.

Our Approach

We aim to always meet client expectations and deliver exceptional value for money, no matter the scale of job being undertaken, from home call outs to large commercial premises. We ensure that we offer great value for money by keeping a tight control of our overheads and we can do this due to having such a large range of skills within our partnership. If a job requires specialist skills we do not have in house, we have a trusted network of contractors, built up over our decades of operating in the region, that we call upon. We operate on a lean basis, keeping only absolute essentials ‘in stock’ and business premises kept to the bare minimum, so do not need to pass on operating costs to our clients.

We look to form long lasting relationships with our clients, so that when they have new work they immediately look to call upon us. To become trusted suppliers we therefore must always deliver on expectations.

Our Charges

Payment terms for fixed price work is 25% in advance (to cover material costs), 25% at a further agreed project point and 50% on completion.

General labour is charged at £60 per man hour.

For gas works labour is charged at £80 per man hour.

We're happy to provide fixed quotes for residential as well as commercial work, just get in touch with details of your requirements on jobs@ribarservices.co.uk and we'll get back to you with a quote.

All prices are subject to VAT.
