Home Renovations

House renovation in South West London can be daunting given the prices some of the larger firms will quote you. With a more streamlined workforce though, you can save significant money and still get the finish you’re after. When taking on a home renovation work, we can manage the project for or with you, or just be part of a team you have put together yourself. If managing for you, we will bring together a small team of other tradesmen as and when necessary during the project (for example, a dedicated plasterer for large plastering work, a dedicated bricklayers etc).

We love working on full renovation projects, it is incredibly satisfying to see a home transformed and we take great pride in the work. We would love to be given the opportunity to work on yours for you – for projects on this sort of scale, we can travel further than usual so don't hesitate to enquire even if you're outside our usual catchment area.

To get the ball rolling, give us a call or email us. The next step would be to discuss over the phone your project & timescale. A visit is then usually warranted before giving you a quote and hopefully starting work. For renovation projects, an up front fee (of 10-20%, depending on project start costs) would be required, and we’d agree a payment plan that triggered payments when certain stages of the development were reached, with a final payment due on completion.
